The Setup

The party + everyone else is at the Castle Ravenloft engaged in dress rehearsals for the upcoming civil war play. The night starts with the usual easy laughter and camaraderie of people that know each other well…untill a few hours later where a scream splits the night sky and a gristly murder is discovered to have occured. The doors slam shut as the magical defenses of the castle accidentally trigger, stopping any entry or exit. our cast of characters are now left to their own devices to quickly discover the killer before any more of the group meet an untimely demise…

the ballroom was locked, not magically, and it was to open at a set time via clockwork to allow everyone to start the rehearsal. the scene is discovered by Rodney
