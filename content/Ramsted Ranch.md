## Lore

#### Description
A large long Ranch house near the north side of Ramsted. Notably, this is the same side of the city that houses the Government District as well as the [[Ramsted GCS Parish|Church Building]]. The building is surrounded by farmlands, all of which are owned by the ranch itself. There is a specific path from the northern city gate to the ranch. This path is a bit more well packed than the supposed main path from which it branches. a lone skinny path goes deeper into the heart of the Ranch, with a wrought iron fence overgrown with hedges shrouding the inside of the compound from the outside.
