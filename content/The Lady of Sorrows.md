A cambion that infiltrated or perhaps raised a death cult in [[The Hundred Hills]] with the help express hopes of destroying the [[Ramsted]] area. 
This was done in anger and sorrow at her child being stollen from her through legal trickery and subterfuge at the hands of the proprietors of [[Ramsted Ranch]]. 

Having accidentally sold her child into a lifetime of slavery and abuse at the hands of rich old pedos she resolved to burn it all down. 

She was a cambion of some note amongst the employ of [[Belial]] (as can be noted by her ability to summon [[I’bies]], the Balor) 

Her child is named [[Jauntief]] and is currently a serving boy in the [[Ramsted Ranch]].
