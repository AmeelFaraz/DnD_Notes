## Features

**Spellcasting.** Kin'ra is a 10th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). he has the following druid spells prepared:

**Cantrips (at will):** Druidcraft, Guidance, Poison Spray, Shillelagh  
**1st level (4 slots):** Absorb Elements, Cure Wounds, Entangle, Goodberry  
**2nd level (3 slots):** Heat Metal, Lesser Restoration, Pass Without Trace, Spike Growth  
**3rd level (3 slots):** Call Lightning, Conjure Animals, Dispel Magic, Plant Growth  
**4th level (3 slots):** Blight, Grasping Vine, Polymorph, Stone Shape  
**5th level (2 slots):** Contagion, Greater Restoration, Insect Plague, Scrying

**Wild Shape (2/short rest).** Kin'ra can use his action to magically assume the shape of a beast with a CR of 1 or lower for up to 5 hours. he can revert to her normal form as a bonus action.

**Circle of Decomposition.** Kin'ra has mastered a controversial branch of druidic magic focused on decay, waste, and the natural cycle of decomposition. he gains the following abilities:

**Natural Recycler.** Kin'ra is immune to disease and poison. he can consume spoiled or rotten food without ill effect and can cast Purify Food and Drink at will without expending a spell slot.

**Fecal Familiar.** Kin'ra is accompanied by a **Dung Beetle Swarm** (use Swarm of Insects stats, but it has 45 HP and immunity to poison damage/condition). The swarm follows his commands and acts on his initiative. If destroyed, he can summon a new one after a long rest.

**Compost Heap.** When Kin'ra casts a spell that deals poison damage or creates difficult terrain, he can choose to make that area produce a noxious stench. Creatures that start their turn in the area or enter it for the first time must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the start of their next turn.

## Actions

**Multiattack.** Kin'ra makes two attacks with his Staff of the Dunghill or uses Fecal Bolt twice.

**Staff of the Dunghill.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit (with Shillelagh), reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 9 (1d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 4 (1d8) poison damage.

**Fecal Bolt.** _Ranged Spell Attack:_ +9 to hit, range 60 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 13 (3d8) poison damage. If the target fails a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, it is also blinded until the end of its next turn as waste matter splashes in its eyes.

**Summon Waste Elemental (1/day).** Kin'ra summons a **Filth Elemental** in an unoccupied space within 30 feet. The elemental is friendly to Kin'ra and her allies, obeys her commands, and acts on her initiative. It remains for 1 hour, until reduced to 0 hit points, or until Kin'ra dismisses it as a bonus action.

**Circle of Fertilization (Recharge 5-6).** Kin'ra targets a point she can see within 90 feet. A 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point fills with brown-green mist. The area is heavily obscured and counts as difficult terrain. Each creature that starts its turn in the mist must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, taking 3d8 poison damage and becoming poisoned for 1 minute on a failed save, or half damage and no poison on a success. Poisoned creatures can repeat the save at the end of each of their turns. The mist lasts for 1 minute or until dispersed by strong wind.

## Bonus Actions

**Wild Shape (2/short rest).** Transform into a beast (CR 1 or lower).

**Septic Command.** Kin'ra commands his Dung Beetle Swarm or Filth Elemental to take an action.

**Fungal Growth.** Kin'ra causes mushrooms to rapidly sprout in a 10-foot square within 60 feet. The area becomes difficult terrain. Any creature that enters the area for the first time or starts its turn there takes 2d6 poison damage.

---

## Reactions

**Absorb Elements (1st-level spell).** When Kin'ra takes acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage, he can use his reaction to reduce the damage and store some of the energy for his next melee attack.

**Excremental Shield.** When Kin'ra is hit by a melee attack, he can use her reaction to coat the attacker with waste matter. The attacker must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned until the end of their next turn.

## Filth Elemental

_Large elemental, neutral_

**AC** 14, **HP** 90 (12d10 + 24), **Speed** 30 ft., burrow 20 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|18 (+4)|10 (+0)|15 (+2)|6 (-2)|10 (+0)|7 (-2)|

**Damage Resistances:** Acid, Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from nonmagical attacks  
**Damage Immunities:** Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Exhaustion, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned, Unconscious  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Tremorsense 30 ft., Passive Perception 10  
**Languages:** Terran (understands but doesn't speak)

**Noxious Presence.** Creatures that start their turn within 5 feet of the elemental must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the start of their next turn.

**Siege Monster.** The elemental deals double damage to objects and structures.

**Actions:**

**Multiattack.** The elemental makes two slam attacks.

**Slam.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) poison damage.

**Engulf (Recharge 5-6).** The elemental moves up to its speed. While doing so, it can enter Large or smaller creatures' spaces. Whenever it enters a creature's space, the creature must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. On a success, the creature can move to the nearest unoccupied space. On a failure, the creature takes 10 (3d6) poison damage and is engulfed. An engulfed creature is blinded, restrained, and takes 10 (3d6) poison damage at the start of each of the elemental's turns. An engulfed creature can escape by using an action to make a DC 15 Strength check. On a success, it escapes to an unoccupied space within 5 feet.
