A young paladin who wants to do what is right, yet his decisions muddy the clarity of that instinct more and more. He answered [[Delmar Danth|Danth's]] summons as he heard about the involvement of necromancy and disease, reminding him of memories about his father, both the bad and the good.

#### Other
Thawaa finds a [[Patron’s Shards| mysterious shard]] in a cave when looking for the body of science. connecting with it, he meets an unknown entity that promises him power beyond his wildest dreams and the ability to achieve everything he could ever want, so long as he gives himself over to it. Thawaa accepts, becoming a servant of [[Mysterious Patron]]. 

The Patron instructs him to recover more of the [[Patron’s Shards]], imbuing thawaa with increasing slivers of power which grant him a dark ability to suck the life out of those he touches. it improves the more shards he collects. 

Face to face with the Doctor of Plagues and Diseases [[Ivebinr Aisendead]], Thawaa faces a reckoning. in his most desperate moment his patron steps in, and ask him for a sacrifice to prove his level of loyalty. Thawaa decides his strength is not enough, and if something is faulty or lacking then it should be thrown away. he renounces his own strength and commits fully to relying on his patron. In doing so, his body becomes a wizened husk of what it was, his skin becoming sallow and loose on his body as his muscles decay. He is left looking skeletal and frail, yet his other sense and abilities blaze with renewed fervor. (+2 to everything other thna strength, strength took a -2 hit)
