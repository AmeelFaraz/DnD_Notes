A mercenary with giants blood running through his veins, along side a smattering of a few other creatures. He joined the party at [[Delmar Danth|Danth's]] promise of riches as well as bloody and dangerous fights.

## Goals
Aside from a general mantra of kill shit get rich, Edon has aligned himself with the plight of the [[Wyrdkin]], and tries to help out [[Immasi]], although the two dont see eye to eye on every matter.
