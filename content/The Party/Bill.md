
A swashbuckling Gith pirate captain. Joined the party as [[Delmar Danth|Danth]] owed him money.

---
## Lore

#### Description
A large hippo-like creature dressed in a dirty red captains jacket and equally soiled shirt. His boots show the many voyages they have undergone and the scars upon his body reveal the outcomes: grim, but survived. should've seen the other guys. He is missing a hand, and has replaced it with a placeholder that allows for several attachments to be mounted to it. almost all are weapons.

#### Characterization
A gruff, no nonsense kind of fellow, he wields two guns and a scimitar

#### Other
Learned how to make pistols from a dwarf, which is also how he learned to speak dwarvish.



---
## Goals

His crew was killed and he was shipwrecked on [[Ercima Centira]] by an [[The Astral Kraken|Astral Kraken]], upon whom he has now sworn revenge. 

Looking for his first-mate, [[Geraldo]] as he suspects him to have played a part in that fateful night on the rainy seas when his ship was rent asunder by the [[The Astral Kraken]]
