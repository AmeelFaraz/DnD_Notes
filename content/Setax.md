Setax is a continent in the world of [[Ercima Centira]]. While there is only 1 land mass, different areas on the mass are referred to as continents. 

It is an anagram of Texas and contains references to this fact 

The main areas of Setax are in the east, with western Setax being much more barren and being seen as rural or a backwater. 

The Capital of Setax is [[Alsadl]], a large urban area with breathtaking mountains and varied elevation 

The settlement of most note in Western Setax is the city of [[Ramsted]].
