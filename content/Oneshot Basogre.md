## Special Basogre Boss Statblock

_Large giant, chaotic evil_

**Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 195 (17d10 + 102)  
**Speed** 40 ft.

---

---

### Saving Throws

**Str** +10, **Con** +10, **Wis** +4

### Skills

**Intimidation** +5, **Perception** +4

### Damage Resistances

Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from nonmagical attacks

### Condition Immunities

Charmed, Frightened

### Senses

Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 14  
**Languages** Common, Giant

**Proficiency Bonus** +4  
**Challenge Rating** 10 (5,900 XP)

---

### Features

**Legendary Resistance (3/day).** If Basogre fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

**Gluttonous Fortitude.** When Basogre would take damage that would reduce him below 50 hit points for the first time, he instead is reduced to 50 hit points and can't be reduced below 50 hit points until the start of his next turn. He then devours a burger from his bag as a free action, letting out a satisfied belch.

**Malding Rage (3/day), but only available after getting below 50% hp.** As a bonus action, Basogre enters a frenzy for 1 minute. While raging:
- He gains advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws
- He deals an extra 1d8 damage with melee weapon attacks
- He has resistance to all damage except psychic damage
- He can't cast spells or concentrate on spells
- His rage ends early if he is knocked unconscious or if his turn ends and he hasn't attacked a hostile creature or taken damage since his last turn

**Meat Shield.** When Basogre is hit by an attack and has a minion within 5 feet of him, he can use his reaction to force the minion to take the hit instead. The minion takes the full damage of the attack.

**Insatiable Hunger.** Basogre has advantage on attack rolls against creatures that are frightened or prone.

**Summon Saminion (Recharge 5-6).** As a bonus action, Basogre summons 1d4 + 1 **Saminion** in unoccupied spaces within 20 feet of him. These saminions act on his initiative count and follow his commands. Saminions disappear after 10 minutes or when reduced to 0 hit points.

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Basogre makes two attacks: one with his greatclub and one with his bite, or he uses Bag of Burgers twice.

**Greatclub.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 19 (3d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

**Bite.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
_Hit:_ 15 (2d8 + 6) piercing damage. Basogre regains hit points equal to half the damage dealt.

**Bag of Burgers (Recharge 4-6).** Basogre reaches into his greasy bag and hurls a burger at a point he can see within 60 feet. Choose one of the following effects:

- **Rancid Burger:** The burger explodes in a 10-foot radius. Each enemy creature in the area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 3d10 poison damage and becoming poisoned for 1 minute on a failed save, or half damage and no poison on a success. Poisoned creatures can repeat the save at the end of each of their turns.
- **Empowering Burger:** The burger lands in a 10-foot radius. Each minion in the area catches and devours a piece, gaining 20 temporary hit points and advantage on attack rolls until the end of Basogre's next turn.
- **Explosive Burger:** The burger detonates on impact. Each creature in a 15-foot radius must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success. The area becomes lightly obscured by grease smoke until the start of Basogre's next turn.

**Crushing Charge (Recharge 5-6).** Basogre moves up to his speed in a straight line and can move through the space of Medium or smaller creatures. Each creature whose space he moves through must make a DC 18 Strength saving throw, taking 4d10 bludgeoning damage and being knocked prone on a failed save, or half damage on a success.

---

### Bonus Actions

**Summon Saminions (Recharge 5-6).** Summon 1d4 + 1 Saminions.

**Aggro For Mae!** Basogre roars a command. All of his minions within 60 feet that can hear him can immediately use their reaction to move up to their speed toward an enemy of Basogre's choice or make one weapon attack.

**Im Malding!!** Basogre flies into a rage.

**Devour Minion.** Basogre grabs and devours one of his minions within 5 feet (no save). The minion is instantly killed and Basogre regains 3d8 + 10 hit points.

---

### Reactions

**Meat Shield.** Force a minion within 5 feet to take a hit meant for Basogre.

**Burger Toss.** When Basogre takes damage from a ranged attack, he can use his reaction to throw a Rancid Burger at the attacker (if Bag of Burgers is available).

---

### Legendary Actions (3/round)

Basogre can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Basogre regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

**Move.** Basogre moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks. Any minions within 10 feet can also move up to half their speed.

**Club Smash.** Basogre makes one greatclub attack.

**Feeding Frenzy (Costs 2 Actions).** Basogre uses Bag of Burgers (if available) to throw an Empowering Burger at his minions, or makes a bite attack.

**Rally the Horde (Costs 3 Actions).** Basogre lets out a terrifying roar and summons 1d3 Saminions in unoccupied spaces within 20 feet of him (regardless of whether Summon saminion is recharged).

## Saminion

_Small humanoid (goblinoid), neutral evil. small bald goblins with exceptionally large noses_


**AC** 13 (leather scraps), **HP** 15 (3d6 + 3), **Speed** 30 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|10 (+0)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|8 (-1)|8 (-1)|6 (-2)|

**Skills:** Stealth +6  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 9  
**Languages:** Common, Goblin

**Nimble Escape.** The minion can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.

**Pack Tactics.** The minion has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the minion's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

**Actions:**

**Rusty Blade.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 5 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage.

**Thrown Rock.** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage.
