A being with highly concentrated amounts of a [[Foundation Deities]] power. 
These beings are the how these deities interact with the multiverse across [[Planes of Existence ]]. 

[[Divine Avatar]]s can bestow aspects of their divinity into other creatures. Gathering enough of a [[Foundation Deities]] power into a mortal will eventually override the mortals owns desires and personality and will convert it into a divine avatar. 

Getting two [[Divine Avatar]]s in close proximity will cause them to meld together(often a cataclysmic event) and if the total amount of divinity is over the set cap, the Deity will be banished for a while and held in contempt of the [[Influence Pact]].
