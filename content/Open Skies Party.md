## Lore

#### Description
The open skies party is lead by [[Stagannis Twistroot]], a large stag that extolls the virtues and necessity of the animals of the forest living in harmony and allowing each animal to have a more equitable existence. the Open Skies party has policy platforms of redistributing the hibernation and forest-oh-one-kay's of some of the larger predator animals as well as animals with larger overall biomass (the neutral squirrel union) to help out those animals that have it harder.

#### Other
While the Open Skies party is localized to [[The Twin Wood]], the party has several sympathetic parties and feeder entities as well as offshoots around the entore ramsted area, including places beyond the swamp.
