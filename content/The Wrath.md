An Ancient Tarrasque. Local tradition holds that the lands around [[Ramsted]] were terraformed or created by [[The Wrath]]‘s terrible power. 

[[The Wrath]] is at the center of a few cults and mystic traditions. It is also sometimes known as Nilbog the Mad. It is a popular choice for local death cults in need of something to revere.

Its calcified body gave rise to [[The Hundred Hills]].

[[The Lady of Sorrows]] attempted to revive [[The Wrath]] with the help of a death cult and a strange arcane construct (which yields a max of 3 [[Kuyobian Keys]] if disassembled correctly)
