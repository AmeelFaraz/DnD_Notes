An ancient and powerful wizard, creator of many magical artifacts and constructs of unknown purpose and power. Some people argue over if Kuyob is even real or if every important magical item of note is automatically prescribed to be his creation due to legend.

---

[[The Grand Wizard Kuyob]] Is an advanced agent of [[The Rodfather]]. He is not quite a [[Divine Avatar]] as he is wary of the limits it would put on his powers, as well as the effects it would have on him (it would wipe his personality and sense of self)

Kuyob is has been working on perfecting his latest creation, [[The White Man]]. A class of construct not yet seen by the world, it is an advanced simulacrum that is capable of reproducing itself and would further Kuyobs own plans on influencing this world, as a means of eventually taking over other worlds. It is not yet known if this a directive of [[The Rodfather]] or something else. 

Kuyob is also the creator and employer of the [[Oiled up Mind Goblins]]. They are technically his avatars, although not made in his image as avatars tend to be. 

Left around the world in various location are [[Kuyobian Keys]]: magical artifacts that can safely open vaults or caches left around the world by [[The Grand Wizard Kuyob]] himself.

Kuyob himself lives in [[Kuyob's Pocket Dimension]], in his [[Tower of Kuyob]]. By virtue of the dimension, he can move his tower around to wherever he wants. However, entering his tower does not guarantee a meeting with the wizard. Aside from him often leaving a simulacrum behind to attend his tower while he is off doing whatever it is that super strong wizards do, he does not wish to waste time dealing with what are usually covetous junior wizards trying to rob him.

Created the homuncular model [[X-J1V]], also known as the jiv (pronounced 'Jeev'). These models have sold well but still suffer from an inability to reproduce which is something Kuyob has been trying to figure out for a while.
