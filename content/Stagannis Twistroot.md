## Lore

#### Description
a large, rather handsome stag with impressive antlers. 
#### Characterization
Stagannis campaigns for the good of all animals, but there is an undercurrent to his speechs and actions that speak to a 'white mans burden'-esque slant to his world view. Since it is well known that the [[Heart of the Forest]] is a stag, or atleast stag-like, Twistroot often echoes sentiment such as 'while it might be the case that I was made in the image of the heart, it doesnt mean that lesser animals deserve to be trampled! we must have compassion and equality for everyone!!'
