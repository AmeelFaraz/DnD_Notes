Known to their friends and family as Eldy and Eldum, these young men are naïve and adventurous and wish to see the world while also helping their family.

---
## Lore

#### Description
Both standing at a medium height for their 16 years of age, their black hair and earnest brown eyes made them seem younger than they were. 
#### Characterization
Fraternal twins, Eldy and Eldum were inseparable from birth. Born as the eldest in a family the eventually came to have 4 younger siblings, they were forced to become the breadwinners of the family after their fathers passing to the death disease and the more mundane yet equally crippling sickness of their mother.
While they knew they had to help their family, they also hungered for adventure. They knew of only 1 possible way to satisfy both needs, which was to join the [[Grand Church of Science]] and attain a good enough rank to be sent out to expeditions. That way, they could earn a living for their family as well as seeing what the world had to offer. 
#### Other
The brothers met [[Alayna Clorvus]] in the streets of the [[Ramsted Slums]]. They befriended her and while they knew there was more to her story as someone like her and the clothes she wore were not common in the slums, they offered companionship without any invading questions. Eventually, they all decided to make for the church, for their own personal reasons.

Following their achievement of the rank of [[Grand Church of Science Hierarchy#Seekers of the Grand Church of Science|Seeker]], the boys joined with [[Bill]], [[Edon]], [[Thaw'aa]], and [[Yobi]] to go on their first quest for science. As they had never left the city prior to this, they were incredibly enamored by [[Edon]] and the fantastical tales he told of his mercenary adventures throughout the land.

Tragedy struck in the fight at the kobold cave as Eldy was overwhelmed in the fighting. [[Bill]], having suffered enough of the children's ramblings this trip decided to do Eldy a mercy and kill him to send him to his brother....

As Eldum lay dying in the cavern floor, his consciousness slipping away, he felt a pull. Still desperate, with the terror of losing his brother and the fear and shame of returning to his family bearing the news of death and failure, he reached out uncaring for what the source may be. *"Hmmm. how very interesting..."* he heard, followed by ominous laughter as his vision finally faded to black.
His eyes flew open seemingly not half a second later. He felt a deep aching emptiness within him...and a sharp, cutting hunger for revenge that accursed animal with the guns, and the other creatures in his miserable party. He would rend their souls from their bodies and feast upon them. He would glory in their viscera and pulpy screams! As Eldum slowly wafted upright, a small part of him couldnt help but feel that perhaps something had gone very, very wrong...
