The Progenitor and Spirit of [[The Twin Wood]]/Wilderness in and around the [[Ramsted]] area.

---
## Lore

#### Description
A giant 20ft tall, translucent silvery-green Elk. Followed around by a herd of similarly ghostly Cervidae of a more normal size.
#### Characterization
Incredibly aloof, the Heart does not particularly care much for the activities of the mortals. However, it will be quite irritated with the state of the necromancer stuff that is being introduced within the wilderness
#### Other
The Heart of the Forest is the deity to which all forest animals are beholden to, and several groups such as the [[Cloven Council]] are  sworn to ensure the protection of.

The Heart of the Forest, similar to [[The Wrath]] is an entity with a large concentration of [[The Essence]]. 

---
## Goals
The goals of the Heart of the Forest are unknown to mortals. Legends say that it wishes to ensure the freedom for all wild things to live out their lives.
