The impressionable young daughter of the Clorvus family. Her childish temperament and naiveté have resulted in quite a few perilous situations for those around her.

## Lore

#### Description
A girl of around 12-14 years of age, Alayna has slightly curly blond hair with wide innocent hazel eyes. she is short of stature and often leaves her hair undone of any braid or bow. She wears two hairbands on her wrist out of habit. Her dress, although still obviously finery, is in a poor state of repair with the bottom of  the hem and a bit further up soiled. 

Her voice is high pitched and a little sing-songy.
#### Characterization
The only child of [[Jonathan Clorvus]] and [[Madam Clorvus|Jolene Clorvus]], Alayna had a very easy childhood and she loved her parents. Spoiled with attention and gifts her whole life, she accepted and expected this as a base reality rather than a privilege many others didn't or couldn't receive. Nonetheless, she was not vindictive or ornery when not given what she thought to be her due but would rather take it as an attack on herself, as though she had done something wrong and was being punished.
Due to being highly sheltered by her parents, she also does not know many children her own age, much less has any friends her own age.
Alayna is also not particularly keen of mind. A bit airheaded, she is very easily convinced of things and highly impressionable, which makes it easy for her to 'get her way' as 'her way' is very often someone else's way that they have simply explained to her as something she should desire. This does have its limits though, as try as her parents might they could not get her to be interested in her various lessons on how to be a proper young lady and learn at least the superficial political intricacies of [[Ramsted]]. Infact, she came to detest politics to a significant degree. 
For all her faults, Alayna has a kind heart. She hates to see people hurting (unless convinced that the 'hurt' is something else ) and will generally try to do as she feels is right.

After a week of tutelage under [[Imponius]], Alayna has gotten fluent in infernal cursing and acting like a thug from Avernus. Although she has actually never been there ever she will totally act as though she has and often talk about the opps she has smoked or the demons she kicks it with (she doesnt do anything of the sort).
- A week of racism indoctrination will not cause this to stop, she will continue to sound like this except she will now also use 'shifter' or 'shifta' freely and make side comments about how wyrdkin are violent or savage or drop the classic cherry picked statlines like 13%-50%. she will still be earnest about it and not malicious, shes copying what shes been taught.
#### Other
In a fit of self-flagellating despair (and likely boredom), Alayna ran away from her home after BOTH her parents refused to sit and play with her again instead of going to another dull boring meeting about that horrid [[Grand Church of Science|Church]]. This was the third time they refused her this month! Undoubtedly they hated her now.
Alayna found herself wandering through the streets, winding up in a strange part of town she had never been in before. Eventually, she found two lovely young men who finally asked her about what she was doing and why she was here. they claimed the [[Ramsted Slums|Slums]] were not safe for her and that they would help her. Introducing themselves as [[The Brothers Tweed]], they invited her back home where they explained their plans to join the [[Grand Church of Science]] to help with their problems at home. As Alayna had problems at home of her own (and out of the tiniest bit of rebellion) Alayna decided to go to the Church along with them.
A dizzying few weeks at Church left Alayna convinced that her parents along with everyone else in the city and likely a few people in the church itself as corrupt and in need of purification that only she could facilitate. Although, surely her oldest friends weren't that bad right? this lapse in her ritual self isolation allowed the party to come in and eventually interrupt her, right when she was about to truly become one with science! she kicked and screamed as the giant barbarian pulled her from the the front of the massive metal double doors that were to slowly open and envelop her with the sight of the body of science...
As Alayna came to her senses, she sat in a room of a [[Alayna’s Safehouse|broken down house]] with her friends all around her, arguing amongst each other. they said they had some place to be and left her with a new friend who they said would take care of her. After a week of intense linguistic expansion and intuited experiences gathered from much pantomime and charades, Alayna's friends came back. they seemed quite a bit softer than they had previously, which struck her as a bit odd.
