## Lore

#### Description
The Shaded Leaf party is all about economic growth and pulling yourself up by your bootstraps. They do not appreciate the notion of equality for equalities sake.

## Conflicts/Quests

[[Victor Clawson]] seems to have indicated to [[Mr Scratch]] that there is some degree of predator superiority sentiment in the upper echelons of the Shaded Leaf, although it is yet unclear if this is rife in the party or just a select few, possibly a sub-faction within.

The Shaded Leaf party has officially contracted the party in order to do some 'creative campaigning' on behalf of the Shaded Leaf. This includes staging attacks by goblins in key constituencies and politically assassinating outspoken [[Open Skies Party]] spokesperson, [[Charlie Chirp]].
