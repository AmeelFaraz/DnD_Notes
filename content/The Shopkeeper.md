The shopkeeper is a travelling salesman with extremely powerful wares. He yields his services to any that require them and are willing to pay. 

He is highly advanced agent of [[The Potion Master]] which explains his elusiveness. 

Befriending the Shopkeeper is no easy feat, but winning his loyalty means you can count on him to help if things go particularly bad...
