The Elected leader of [[The Twin Wood]] and general forests around the [[Ramsted]] area

---
## Lore

#### Description

The previous Viceroy was kidnapped by [[The Orme House of Healing]] and was tortured and mutilated for magical parts. He was a wyvern and was the one Mr Scratch's octo-cam caught sight of for a split second as the party escaped from the hospital.

The new viceroy has yet to be elected.
#### Characterization


#### Other

The Viceroy is technically a public servant even though most lord it over the other animals. While their political power is quite significant, it is NOT the only political power of the wilderness, nor is it the single most powerful one. In fact, the position is a carveout made by some enterprising earlier animals that sort of sat on the coattails of the extremely powerful Heart of the Forest and use it to find a place in the conversations regarding the wilderness. other major players in wilderness politics include the [[Cloven Council]] as well as the Fae court of [[Undocumented Poisonous Mire Area|Tau'Sin]] and the [[Coven of Sufayd Aurat]] 

---
## Goals

The main goal of any Viceroy is to maintain the wellbeing of the animals in the Ramsted area as well as ensuring the [[Heart of the Forest]] remains safe and free
