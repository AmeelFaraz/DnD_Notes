## Lore

### Description
The species usually lives in [[The Astral Plane]], of the many [[Planes of Existence]]. In [[The Mortal Plane]] it usually resides in deep space.

## Goals
This particular kraken has been on the run from forces yet unknown, and was drawn to this area of [[Setax]] and [[Ramsted]] in particular as a result of the efforts of [[Necrolord Nyctasha]]. 

It is reaching maturity and will subsequently trigger a [[Maandilaan]], which will cause other krakens to arrive, and attempt to mate with the kraken in a bloody display of dominance and death. 

It has tried to create itself a lair, and after an unsuccessful attempt to wrestle control of a sahaugin settlement due to the creation of an [[Awakened Mother]], it is trying to create another one further along [[The Ramsted Coast]] at the [[Undocumented Poisonous Mire Area]] and [[Naymor's Sepulcher]]. 
## Known Abilities
It created and used an Avatar to exercise its will, something it subconsciously knew how to do due to the concentration of [[The Essence]] it has within it.
