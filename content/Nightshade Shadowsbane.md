The head [[Grand Church of Science Hierarchy#Scalpels of the Grand Church of Science|Scalpel of the Grand Church of Science]]. Like his past, his face is shrouded in shadows. Due to his high levels of secrecy, there is a debate on if he is truly real or just a fanciful tale.

---

## Lore

##### Description
Nightshade Shadowsbane stands at a middling height with an unassuming figure. He wields a wicked dagger and wears several throwing knives and other unknown weapons concealed over his body. He wears loose dark clothing with well made armor both under and on it. His eyes appear as twin glowing white lines floating in the unnerving silhouetted mass that is his head. 
##### Characterization
While his past is truly unknown, the figure known as Shadowsbane does in fact exist. Terse in his manner of speaking and with an affinity for showing rather than telling, he is an efficient and ruthless machine who seems to do exactly as he is told.
However, it is clear to any who have interacted with him that he is no mindless minion content to do whatever his masters decide. While he has yet to disobey one, he questions orders and wishes to know the purpose behind things before he does them. He also takes to training new people/recruits with with what seems to be eagerness and a sort of pleasure. 
#### Other
While Shadowsbane was part of the church, he was corrupted by a ring he found out on one of his missions. The ring granted him the ability to go invisible, but did so at the cost of corrupting his soul and free will. He is now a servant and agent of [[Necrolord Nyctasha]] and has been tasked for a while with finding suitable new candidates for the Necrolord's Horsemen, of which he is the first. Shadowsbane does still maintain at least an echo of his former self as can be seen in the way he questions orders and is still devoted to science (as much as he can be).

## Goals

Due to his soul and mind being that of the Necrolord, he does as desired. Previously, this was recruiting candidates to increase the Necrolords power.

Shadowsbane's current assignment is to patrol the [[Undocumented Poisonous Mire Area]] in case anyone gets close enough to [[Naymor's Sepulcher]] to possibly cause problems and to stop them.
