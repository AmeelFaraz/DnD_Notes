The 11 year old mining foreman at the [[Hundred Hills Mine]]

---
## Lore

#### Description
A young boy, youthful face always covered in the soot from torches and the dust and dirt of the earth in which he toils. He is extraordinarily strong for an 11 year old. 
#### Characterization
Like Erwin from attack on titan, he is a charismatic and inspiring leader as well as a noble paladin of the earth and the ways of the mines. He never backs down and will always help those in need of aide especially if they are those sadly numerous weak and helpless creatures known as adults.
#### Other
Erwin is probably a lot older than 11 but there seems to be some magic involved that keeps him in a perpetual state of youth..

---
## Goals
To stay in the mines and keep mining because the children yearn for the mines.

### Abilities

**Divine Sense (4/long rest).** Erwin can detect the presence of celestials, fiends, and undead within 60 feet until the end of his next turn.

**Lay on Hands (50 HP pool).** Erwin can restore hit points or cure diseases and poisons by touch.

**Divine Smite.** When Erwin hits with a melee weapon attack, he can expend a spell slot to deal additional radiant damage: 2d8 for a 1st-level slot, plus 1d8 for each spell level higher.

**Fighting Style: Great Weapon Fighting.** When Erwin rolls a 1 or 2 on a damage die for an attack with a melee weapon he's wielding with two hands, he can reroll the die (must use new roll).

**Extra Attack.** Erwin makes two attacks when he takes the Attack action.

**Aura of Courage (10 ft.).** Erwin and friendly creatures within 10 feet can't be frightened.

**Divine Health.** Erwin is immune to disease.

**Stone's Endurance (Recharge 5-6).** When Erwin takes damage, he can use his reaction to reduce the damage by 1d12 + 5.

**Earthen Fortitude.** Erwin has resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks while standing on natural earth or stone.

**Sacred Oath: Oath of the Miner.** Erwin has sworn to honor the ancient calling of mining, believing it to be the holiest of all professions. He is bound by his tenets:

- _Dig Deep, Stand Tall:_ Never abandon a mine shaft or a companion
- _Strike True:_ Let each swing of the pickaxe be purposeful and just
- _Forge Forward:_ Never back down simply because the path forward is hard

**Earthen Step.** Erwin can burrow through nonmagical earth and stone as if it were difficult terrain, leaving no tunnel behind. He can use this movement on his turn without using an action.
