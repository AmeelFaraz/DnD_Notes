An incredibly powerful (and subsequently dangerous) entity that requires dark rituals and blood sacrifice to create. 

---
The ritual to create one is a closely guarded secret by the sahaugin priest class and is only attempted in the most dire of situations due to the power the [[Awakened Mother]] has over other sahaugins and sea creatures. 

The [[Awakened Mother]] has mind control abilities that can rival a [[The Astral Kraken]], or at least, the younger version of it that created the Kraken’s Avatar. 

[[Ferina]] was transformed into an Awakened mother in the sauhaugin cavern
