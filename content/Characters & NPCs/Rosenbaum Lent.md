## Lore

#### Description
A relatively portly man, rosenbaum is getting on in his years. His hair and beard along with his large puffy mustache have all begun to grey and whiten, with his hair all but gone and pushed over in a rather sad looking combover. this whole affair generally remains hidden under a hat, usually a small bowler or modest tophat. His face reddens easily and he is not very athletic. 
Generally well dressed in fashions that are slightly outdated, he wears full leather armor with a more substantial breastplate when he dresses up for his conclave job. 
#### Characterization
Lent is self important and pompous. He is also not particularly intelligent and can be covetous and easily duped. He is not easily trusting as much as he is easily flattered, which allows for it to be easy to get one up on him. He can also be made to believe most things that would feed into his long held prejudices. Lent is racist towards [[Wyrdkin]], not like angry racist but looks down and thinks of them as lesser racist. he does not think that wyrdkin can be capable of anything of note and mostly blames them for the death disease and also thinks they probably helped bring down the city government.
#### Other
Lent has no powers beyond those any disgraced politician may have of deflecting questions and shirking responsibility. To help account for this, he has an array of magical items that help him pose as a cleric.

He knows of immasi, mostly as a rabble-rousing firebrand that the city had dealt with back in the day. He truly thinks of the wyrdkins as lesser, so imagines that a firm stance and some corporal punishment will bring them into line. as a result of this, he views [[Immasi's uprising]] as more of a chance to restore government control
