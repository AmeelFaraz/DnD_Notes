## Lore

#### Description
A woman in her early 20s, Ferina is short of stature with long hair that reaches her mid back. Her simple threadbare skirt and blouse reflect her upbringing in a fishing village without many luxuries to spare. Her slightly more defined muscles when compared to other women her age along with a tan denote a harder life where women must pitch in their share of manual labor as well.

After her transformation into an [[Awakened Mother]], Ferina now has gills along the side of her neck, dark black scleras, slight webbing between her finger and toes, and 4 tentacles sprouting from her back (up to 8 can appear). her teeth also have become more sharp and her skin has become paler and tinged with blue. She also still has the symbol carved in the center of her chest still.
#### Characterization
Imaginative and free from a young age, Ferina had to become responsible from a young age due to the hardships in the village. In order to make money for their family, Ferina's father would would head to [[Ramsted]] almost every day to sell fish and bycatch. The family became increasingly dependent on Ferina catching fish for them for her father to then go and sell a ways away at [[Ramsted]]. Nevertheless, she enjoyed life and loved her family, particularly her younger sister whom she spoiled and tried to provide as idyllic and carefree a life as possible. Indeed the faded pink ribbon her sister wears is a testament to Ferina's nature, as she had given up the one bit of ornamentation she owned to bolster someone else's happiness. 
However, Ferina's sacrificial nature does not mean she allows herself to be walked over, as people quickly found out. When pushed, she is firm and is prone to bouts of anger when particularly frustrated. For all her hardiness, Ferina is still sheltered and naïve in certain matters. she has not seen much of the wider world aside from a trip to [[Ramsted]] and is perhaps too trusting at times. She also loves the Sea.
