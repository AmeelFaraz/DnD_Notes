#### Characterization
The main head is slow in speech and thought but a bit more inclined to reason. the smaller head is fast in speech and thought and much more aggressive and instinctual. Neither is particularly intelligent and it is not too hard to trick them. 
neither head really knows how to count.
#### Other
Faifil stopped terrorizing the local villages after being convinced by [[Silas]] that adults were much better meals because they were larger and could be cooked to enhance the flavors. He traded Faifil a mango chutney recipe in exchange for leaving in one piece with many promises of how great it was.
