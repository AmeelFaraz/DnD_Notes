The Mysterious Patron is [[Thaw'aa]]’s patron. The entity is malevolent in nature as it delights in the pains of others and views absolute power as the only real truth in the world. 

---
## Lore 
%%The patron is some type of death god lich thing. The end result is going to be making it like the lich, with the [[Patron’s Shards]]

The Patron was defeated a long time ago by some other party which scattered its powers and influence. it may have actually done so on purpose after having gotten too close to becoming a [[Divine Avatar]]. perhaps Kuyob aided those who destroyed it in the past as well.

It also crafty, and excels at manipulating creatures and people to do its bidding. While it has had interactions with people in the past, it claims to have ‘missed’ interacting with humans.

It knows of [[The Grand Wizard Kuyob]] and views him as a massive threat, as his power is equivalent to his own when at full capacity. #privated %%

## Goals
It has stated to [[Thaw'aa]] that it wants him to find and combine [[Patron’s Shards]] that are scattered around the area. In exchange, the Mysterious Patron promises untold power as a reward. 

%%The Mysterious Patron often presents itself to other entities with concentrations of [[The Essence]], and attempts to get them to create large scale death events. the promises are different to each person, however it seems that most are told that they will become his chosen and inherit his power or something similar.

The Mysterious Patron is a High Lich/Death God. it wants people or creatures to cause death and destruction so that it can grow in power. It also has knowledge of the [[Foundation Deities]] and knows what will happen if it takes up too much of [[The Essence]]. As such, it hopes to control as many comparatively weaker beings as possible yet still being strong enough to further its world domination to eventually go interplanetary. 

It wants a body again which it is grooming Thawaa to become #privated %%
