An Imp, sworn to the service of [[Mammon]] and currently in the employ of [[Mr Scratch]].

---
## Lore

#### Description
Being an Imp, Imponius can shapeshift into quite a few different shapes. Chief amongst these (and the form he identifies with the most) is that of a classic imp, standing at approximately 3ft tall with small red leathery bat wings and two black horns jutting from his forehead, curving backwards slightly. 
#### Characterization
Having been gainfully ~~enslaved~~ employed for an uncountable number of years, Imponius knows a great deal about much of the workings of the Hells but is bored and has an uninterested attitude to most things
#### Other
His favorite food is Blood Candied Nuts

alter ego Birdonius

## Goals

Imponius's goals are bound to his master and as such he shares whatever goals his master has.

aside from contractual obligations, imponius seems to be saving whatever meager gold and money he receives for some purpose.

---
## Conflicts/Quests

Imponius is wanted by the [[Grand Church of Science]] due to his involvement as the titular Lustcubus in the Lustcubus Incident

He has befriended [[Alayna Clorvus]] and at Mr Scratch's request has taught her to hate the [[Grand Church of Science]] in order to undo the brainwashing she incurred.
