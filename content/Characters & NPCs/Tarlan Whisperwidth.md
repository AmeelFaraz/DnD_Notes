## Lore

#### Description
Standing at 9 feet tall and broad of frame, Tarlan strikes an imposing figure. While mostly humanoid, his large horns and broad nose complete with a ring give away his [[Wyrdkin]] status. His perpetually rolled up sleeves show off his muscular forearms which many could swear have a cup and rag at the ends rather than hands. He wears a clean white shirt that has gotten yellower with age and simple rough spun brown pants. 
#### Characterization
Very gruff and a man of few words when he speaks. His voice is deep. mostly snorts unless pushed very hard. He prefers hard honest work rather than a life of many luxuries. While he was much more wild and spirited in his youth, he is a lot more reserved and of the 'protect your own' mindset in his current age. while he is not hard hearted, he truly believes that everyone earns their karma and does not weep for friends that die in service of what they desire.
#### Other
An ex-adventurer, he is one of [[Delmar Danth]]'s many connections and could have been called up by him for a quest if things had gone differently.

He is a strong druid and bard
