A Dwarven Master Blacksmith. He is one of the few beings to be let into the [[Azer Holy Forge]] based on his skill and knowledge of shaping metals.  

Bram can create advanced armors if given the proper materials. he can also upgrade weapons.  He is also one of the only people in the [[Ramsted]] area who could create gilded door hinges for the [[Doors of Kuyob]]. 

He has hidden himself away in a small village to the east of [[Ramsted]].  He did so as a result of finding out that the mine he had worked in for so long was actually a tarrasque corpse instead of the earth. as such, he is now depressed that the great lore of his people of the magical trove of metal and glory deep in the mountains must be false. the party will have to invigorate bram to become a smith once more and take up his hammer if they want his help.
