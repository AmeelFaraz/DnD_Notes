The strong-willed and keen leader of the [[Wyrdkin]]. While his past is shrouded in mystery, what is evident and obvious is that he will get what he perceives as justice for his people, through any means necessary. 

---
>*"I will not rest till every drop of wyrdkin blood has been repaid with interest"*
## Lore

#### Description
A 6ft tall and leanly muscular WereRat, Immasi wears two longswords at his sides and a dagger at his back. Besides the cloth pants and leather sword belts that adorn his bottom half, the only armor he wears is a metal shoulder pauldron. Proud of his heritage, he walks around in his hybrid state of a bipedal, anthropomorphic rat.
#### Characterization
[[Immasi]] does not think very highly of human. In fact, he is outright dismissive and largely racist towards them. He is also holds a deep mistrust for them. However, this is likely in reaction to the centuries of abuse and oppression the [[Wyrdkin]] have undergone. Pushed into the [[Ramsted Sewers]], he plots on how to exact his justice upon the hateful surface dwellers that have hurt his people. 
He is shrewd and cunning, and knows that overthrowing the humans in control is not something that can be done overnight, which is why he has spent years preparing his people and gathering resources for this endeavor.

## Goals

[[Immasi]]'s main goal is to achieve control over [[Ramsted]] and the surrounding area, to create a safe haven for wyrdkin everywhere to be able to live.

## Conflict/Quests 

In order to gain [[Immasi]]'s trust, he requires shows of loyalty. After convincing him to not kill any non-[[shifters]], he will ask for 4 things before revealing any details about himself or his plans.

- To begin, Immasi will demand that the party search through the [[Ramsted Sewers]] to locate the [[Basogre's Lair]], and see if [[Basogre]] and his people could be convinced to join Immasi's cause.

Following a successful handling or recruitment of [[Basogre]], Immasi will deem the party useful, and will reveal the three tasks he needs completed before he will reveal his ultimate plan of [[Immasi's uprising]]

- Immasi will request that the party head to [[The Orme House of Healing]] to free the [[Wyrdkin]] trapped within the walls of the hospital, to be used as experiment fodder or ingredients.
	- Immasi has determined through rumors and investigation by one of his lieutenants [[Skem Shearpaws]] that the house of healing is manufacturing their effective medicines by utilizing the wyrdkins often magically imbued bodies/

- Immasi wants the spread of [[Bottled Joy]] to be put to an end, as it is ravaging the ranks of the wyrdkin (who seem extra susceptible to it). 
	- He knows that the [[Bottled Joy]] is peddled by the[[Conclave of Lliira]]. and wants his people mentally and physically free for what is to come.

- Immasi confesses that the [[Wyrdkin]] are not as well armed as he would like. This is due to the lack of metals coming in from [[Hundred Hills Mine]]. He would like for the flow of metals to commence again so that he can put to use some of his people who are capable blacksmiths to make custom weapons for the Wyrdkin. 
	- His assaults on the [[Grand Church of Science]]'s weapon shipments have been getting thwarted repeatedly, which leads him to think that there is a [[wyrdkin mole]] in his midst. this has lead to Immasi getting more paranoid and harsh, possibly costing him some trust in followers that were already uneasy with his leadership as is.

- Immasi wants to know what the other factions know about the wyrdkin and how ready they are for the coming assault. He would like to see what the party could discover
	- Immasi has excellent intelligence and already knows what the other factions (mostly just the [[Conclave of Lliira]] and the [[Grand Church of Science]]) know. he just wants to see how trustworthy the party is.

After all quests are done or ignored within the time frame (2 weeks in game time), Immasi will launch [[Immasi's uprising]] in [[Ramsted]], which will be a major point in the story/campaign with major consequences in how things operate in the city.

Immasi has a [[Patron’s Shards|Patron Shard]] that can be collected after doing 3 of his quests or killing him

Immasi is explicitly at odds with the [[City Government]], however he is not factoring them into his plans as he doesn't think they are enough left to matter. he does know of [[Jonathan Clorvus]], [[Rosenbaum Lent]], [[Tom Thedrow]], and [[Venka Arton]] but not in their newer 'rebel' or 'outcast' capacity. He also does not know much regarding [[Madam Clorvus]] or her organization efforts. This is largely because he doesn't believe the racist and uppity humans would ever come down into the sewers which lets them get away with operating in the sewers under Immasi's nose. 

Immasi is getting money and food from [[Ramsted Ranch]] as he needs to keep his people fed somehow. They have so far only extracted promises of safety but Immasi knows that there are strings attached to this. The Ranch gives Immasi supplies in exchange for him kidnapping human(or humanoid, whatever) orphans for the ranch. Immasi has agreed to this on the condition that the wyrdkin children would not be touched. This agreement has been put in jeopardy by the Ranch revealing that they were the ones that hired the party all along.
