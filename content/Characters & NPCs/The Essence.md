One of the [[Foundation Deities]]. It, and its substance, are the core of all magics in the multiverse. However, it requires creation to and cultivation to truly exist and it is just as often half sentient as it is sentient. This is due in part to the speed at which it will gather its influence and concentrate its power into beings. It is also capable of concentrating its power into several things across worlds at once, thought to be a side effect of the fact that basically every artifact or concentration of magic is actually just a concentration of [[The Essence]]. 

It is hungry yet possessive of what was made from it, which is basically most things. Creatures with high concentration of [[The Essence]] will often lean more and more into baser instincts and have incredible power.
