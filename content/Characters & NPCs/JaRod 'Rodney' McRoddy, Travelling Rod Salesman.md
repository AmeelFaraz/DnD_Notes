## Lore

#### Description
Dressed in fine silk and edged in furs, Rodney wears a flashy purple suit, along with purple shoes and a purple hat. his purple cravat is stylishly tucked into his purple vest, which covers his fine white shirt. He always carries a rod with him, using it as a cane almost.
#### Characterization
He is very greedy and shifty, but also tricky and flamboyant especially when making deals with people, which brings him much pleasure.
