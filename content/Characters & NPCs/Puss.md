## Lore

#### Description
A large black hat, grown slightly floppy around the brim with just a tinge of patina on the velvet shows the age and adventures this hat and the particular adventurer it belongs to have undergone. Its wide expanse shades the sharp emerald eyes of the wily, orange furred tabaxi that wears it. Clad in swashbuckling boots and loose baggy trousers with a feather in his hat and a vest on his back this character oozes the self confident charm of a professional snake oil salesman. 

#### Characterization
Puss loves gambling, it is a vice that can often get the better of him. He is a cheat at cards though. While he has no interest in doing heroics and will readily rob people, he does have a sense of morality which will cause him to scam the less fortunate to a lesser degree. 
Puss will also very easily let others sacrifice themselves for him. 

He is a free spirit, not caring for [[Immasi]]‘s call to action to all [[Wyrdkin]] and thinks that the idea of [[Immasi's uprising]] would be bad for business. Of course, his business is scamming and stealing from people but who’s counting ? 
#### Other
He is the leader of [[Puss’s Crew]]. Generally not interested in religion, he is superstitious about [[Lady Luck]] in hopes of getting better gambling wins.

### Features

**Feline Agility.** When Puss moves on his turn in combat, he can double his speed until the end of the turn. Once he uses this trait, he can't use it again until he moves 0 feet on one of his turns.

**Cat's Claws.** Puss has retractable claws that deal 1d4 + 5 slashing damage. These are natural weapons.

**Cat's Talent.** Puss has proficiency in Perception and Stealth.

**Cunning Action (Rogue).** Puss can take a bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide.

**Sneak Attack (5d6).** Once per turn, Puss deals an extra 5d6 damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally that isn't incapacitated and Puss doesn't have disadvantage.

**Uncanny Dodge.** When an attacker Puss can see hits him with an attack, he can use his reaction to halve the attack's damage.

**Evasion.** When Puss is subjected to an effect that allows him to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, he instead takes no damage on a success and half damage on a failure.

**Action Surge (1/short rest).**

**Second Wind (1/short rest).** 1d10 + 10 hit points.

**Fighting Style: Dueling.** When wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons, Puss gains +2 to damage rolls (already included).

**Extra Attack.** Puss can attack twice, instead of once, whenever he takes the Attack action on his turn.

---

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Puss makes two attacks with his rapier.

**Rapier.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 11 (1d8 + 7) piercing damage.

**Dagger (Thrown).** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +9 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 7 (1d4 + 5) piercing damage.

**Kitty Eyes (3/day).** Puss locks eyes with one creature within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be Charmed until the end of his next turn. If the target fails by 5 or more, Puss can choose what they do for their next action.
