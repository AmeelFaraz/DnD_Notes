## Lore

#### Description
Byron is the larger of the two brothers but is also younger. he is heavily muscled and has short buzzed hair with a wide loose smile that can surprisingly look very menacing at certain angles

Myron is smaller and much less muscled with a longer hair and ill-groomed facial hair.

their clothes vary extremely depending on what con they are currently running
#### Characterization
The pair are very different from each other, almost opposites. Myron is more conniving and thinks up the plans and heists. Byron is more than happy to lumber along behind his older brother and do as he is told. myron often berates his stupid younger brother, but he does love him deep down. Byron takes the abuse because he knows thats just how his brother is. Myron is also fidgety and flighty while byron is more grounded and seemingly lazier.

#### Other
The brothers are now part of [[Mr Scratch]]'s company now after being coerced into signing a contract written in blood
