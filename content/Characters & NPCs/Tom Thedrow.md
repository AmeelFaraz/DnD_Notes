## Lore

#### Description
A drow with a rough stubble, bristly mustache and a squarish face. His ears are fat and only have the barest point up at the peak, leading for him to be often mistaken for a darkskinned human. A rough edged scar goes down over his left eye, and his ridged posture makes him look like one you would rather not cross.
#### Characterization
Has the exaggertaed british accent, oi mate u avin a jerk mate

Aggressive and prone to action rather than lofty talk, Tom is also suspicious and not very quick to trust. An accomplished fighter from his childhood spent on the streets, he prefers to get in peoples faces rather than threaten to do so.
However, he is prone to flattery and can be swayed by buttering him up enough. 
#### Other
Tom is an accomplished spy and fairly good at it, all things considered. he was stationed in the church for a while and has gathered intelligence for the government on the things the church has been up to. He has leaked that the church is also looking for [[Deacon Clein]] and that [[Deacon Dahc]] is currently in over his head trying to keep the church in order. He did not realize alayna was at the church as during important ceremonies and things he was sneaking off to the deacons offices looking for information.

After Clorvus, he was [[Delmar Danth]]'s best friend in the government. Not super bent up over danths death as he isnt much scared of death.
