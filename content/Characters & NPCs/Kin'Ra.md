## Lore

#### Description
A ropemaxxing hobgoblin, Kin'Ra appears more like a halfling than a hobgoblin, something that gets him into trouble at times when he goes about his buisness in the woods, as he doesnt like harming anything, even if they have him at swordpoint.

#### Characterization
Kin'ra is generally good natured and kind, although he is ecentric and deeply involved with his 'studies'. He will grow furious if he witnesses someone harming an animal infront of him, which is one of the ways to witness him truly flex the lengths of his arcane abilities.

## Goals

Kin'Ra is a great defender of animals but also wants to study them as he is a researcher as well. He hopes to collect the feces from every type of animal around to help him create the definitive naturalist text  of his time.

Kin'Ra is also a charged protector of the [[Heart of the Forest]] as a member of the [[Cloven Council]].
