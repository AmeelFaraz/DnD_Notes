## Lore

#### Description
A large man of prodigious girth, his clammy yellowed skin is covered with thin scars that criss cross his lumpy body. His skin is loose in places and tight in the other, almost seeming stitched together. His bald head is set with two small beady black eyes that occasionally betray a glimpse of a green corona within them.  his large flat mouth displays a smattering of teeth beset at odd angles within it. Golomon wears a tattered apron that might have once been white, and a pair of breeches in a similar state of slovenly neglect and disrepair. 
Golomon pushes around a large two wheeled cart upon which he places the soon-to-be-interred. He keeps a large cleaver hanging from his belt and a odd large hook on his upper left arm, linked with a length of chain that wraps around his girth as a belt of sorts.
#### Characterization
Golomon is not particularly bright, but he is not a simpleton either. he delights in cruelty and is lazy and callous towards others. contrary to most with his unfortunate dental situation, his mouth seems perpetually curved in a downwards smile. He speaks in fits and bursts, his words and sentiments often off-putting and crude. He is also a bit of a shifty individual, cagey with questions and suspicious of those that ask too many.
