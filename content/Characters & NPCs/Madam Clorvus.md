Jolene 'Madam' Clorvus is the current head of the ex-[[City Government]]. She took over after the death of her husband, [[Jonathan Clorvus]]. She is [[Alayna Clorvus]]'s mother.

---
## Lore

#### Description
Once the envy of women and the object of several men's desires, father time has begun to catch up with Madam Clorvus. While she is by no means an old crone, she cannot be described as youthful any longer. Her dark black hair has begun to silver and the lines have begun to appear on her visage, perhaps accelerated by the recent stress she has undergone. Her eyes remain as keen as ever though, noting the intellectually inclined mind behind them. 
#### Characterization
Madam Clorvus was long content to enjoy the finer things in life, which her husband provided for her by virtue of his position in the government. She also enjoyed the occasional adrenaline pulsing romp of acting the innocent bimbo to spy for him. She preferred not to bother herself with the more mundane and likely boring minutia of actually governing the city and lifestyle she enjoyed. Perhaps if she had been more attentive in these matters she wouldn't have found herself caught completely unaware by the acute downward spiral of the [[City Government]] at the hands of [[Deacon Clein]]. A testament to the ability of even her willfully unexercised intellect, she managed to piece together the truth of what was going on and stopped the four most powerful leaders of the government [[Tom Thedrow]], [[Venka Arton]], [[Rosenbaum Lent]] and her own husand [[Jonathan Clorvus]] from killing each other over Clein's manipulations. However, it was too little too late and the illustrious and long held government of [[Ramsted]] had collapsed.
#### Other
After getting the goverment leaders back on the same page, her and her husband worked together to try to regain the trust of the people and undermine the church. In doing so, they perhaps neglected their young spoiled daughter. After the targeted assassination of her husband, she has fled into the [[Ramsted Sewers]] to protect herself and the fledgling city church resistance.

While she is no fan of [[Wyrdkin]] or particularly sympathetic to their cause, she is not racist against them and does think they should have been treated better.

She doesn't actually know what goes on in [[Ramsted Ranch]] and thinks it is probably just some strip club and gambling joint. she doesnt want her leaders to be associated with the corruption and waste of the past and would be mad because of that (if she were to hear they were there recently).

Madam Clorvus would like to set up the City Goverment again, and also wants revenge against the church (and particularly deacon clein).

She also wants to find her daughter again and keep her safe as she is all that is left of her family
