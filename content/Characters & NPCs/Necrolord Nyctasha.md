A necromancer that has been quietly amassing immense power and influence behind the scenes. He contains a high amount of [[The Essence]] within him. 

He is behind the spread of the [[Death Disease]] in the land by locating and imitating the [[Curse of Naymor]] and has a hand in or been a driving force behind almost every major event or conflict in the Ramsted area to further his cause (and the ritual). 

## Goals 
Nyctasha desires power. As he is a necromancer, this leads to the logic conclusion of trying to trigger as much death as possible to expand his dominion. However, the Necrolord knows that having too much of [[The Essence]] within him would cause problems, and [[Divine Avatar]]s of [[The Essence]] dissolve all too often. As such, he is doing what most being of sufficient knowledge of the workings of the universe do, which is to maintain a certain level of intrinsic power and surround yourself with powerful beings or creatures you can control. 
The [[Curse of Naymor]] is part of this, where its use would allow him to supplant or consume the current strongest Essence Creature in the area, [[Mysterious Patron]]. 

Once he has taken [[Mysterious Patron]]’s place, he will then send forth his armies across the land, killing and resurrecting people till he is the sole power on the planet.
