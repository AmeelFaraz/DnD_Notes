## Lore

#### Description
a mousy looking man, he is tall but often mistaken for being short as he spends much of his time hunched over and near corners. His constant hand wringing and nervous attitude have lent him a haunted visage with dark bags under his eyes. 
#### Characterization
Venka would describe himself as a realist with a love for being prepared. most others would just call him a coward. His plans often boil down to running away and hoping things sort themselves out. as unflattering as it may be for ones image, it has worked for him in many ways throughout his life and he does manage to avoid many tragedies this way.
