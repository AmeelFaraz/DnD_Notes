## Lore
#### Description
A man of middling height and longer, greasy hair. His skin is sallow and pale while his eyes seem almost permanently narrowed. He keeps his mouth pressed into a hard line. His white cloak of deaconhood, while not perfectly clean at all times is nevertheless seemingly always wrinkle-free and pressed. His left eye has a slight twitch to it at times.
#### Characterization
Although he has a sizeable measure of power and was one of the strongest (arguably THE strongest figure) in [[Ramsted]], Clein is a petty and childish man with a cruel streak. the quintessential bully, he enjoys punching down and panics if pressed or openly and directly confronted. He builds an outside persona of being cold and calculating while those close to him know him to be petulant and known to hold grudges. He is a sore loser and a bad sport. 
While he may not be a smart as he would like to be believed, he IS conniving and has gotten fairly good at getting an edge out over people which is how he arrived at his position in the first place. Indeed, there are rumors of knives-in-the-dark surrounding Clein's ascension to Deaconhood. For all his faults, Clein is still effective at his job.
#### Other
Cleins corruption is tacitly allowed/ignored by the [[Grand Church of Science Hierarchy#Archbishop of the Grand Church of Science|Archbishop]] [[Willium Nylus]] as Clein does still provide the church with funding and keep some important operations running. However, this results in [[Deacon Dahc]] being at odds with Clein, seeing him for the corrupt weasel he truly is. The two posture and needle at each other constantly, with [[Deacon Dahc]] attempting to indict Clein in front of the Archbishop for at least one of his several crimes against the church.

Clein was responsible for the downfall of the [[City Government]]. originally pretending to be a reasonable man and mediating conflicts, he won the trust of the leaders of the government and began to turn them against each other. He also managed to get most of the city to be on the church's side since the government had gone to them to see if they had any cure in the first place.

Recently, Clein has gotten more paranoid and anxious as he seems to believe that he may be being targeted by one of the several outgroups he has maintained contact with or colluded with.

Clein is also the deacon responsible for setting up the [[Tribulations]] as well as overseeing training for the Scalpels. 

He seems to think he has a lot more control over the Scalpels (and particularly [[Nightshade Shadowsbane]]) than he may actually have.

## Goals

Clein wishes to enrich himself and gain as much power as he can. The ulterior motives for this are to allow him to indulge his vices of gambling and women.
