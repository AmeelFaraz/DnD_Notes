## Lore

### Description
A strange contraption made of glass and copper, it look like a gilded pyramid with various gears and shifting pistons within it. when brought near something sealed by Kuyob, the mechanisms within it will begin to pulse and the pyramid will open up, melding with the lock and evaporating both.
