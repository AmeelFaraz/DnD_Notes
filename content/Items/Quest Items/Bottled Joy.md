A mysterious potion being pedaled by the [[Conclave of Lliira]]. Highly addictive, a significant portion of the city has come under its influence.

---

## Lore

#### Description 
A mliky-white fluid that seems to sparkle and swirl inside of its container. While it is odorless and tasteless, it has an effect on the mind that is unmistakable and noticeable as soon as a bottle is opened.

#### Creation Process
[[Conclave of Lliira]] make the base or starting or whatever then they ship it to [[The Orme House of Healing]] for vital extra ingredients which are probably the soul bits of wyrdkins when they got seperated from their bodies to make the [[Amalgamation]]s.

#### Addiction Process
Consuming the potion will trigger a DC15 wisdom save. 
The effects of the potion can be removed with an application of greater restoration
Upon saving, you shrug off the effects of the potion and are simply tipsy and confused for 3 turns, giving disadvantage on wisdom checks. 
Upon first failure, character now has a minor addiction. they will have to make a wisdom save at the start of every day to see if the addiction worsens or not. if they pass the wisdom save, they will incur no negative effects for the day.
Upon the second failure, the addiction will progress. the DC to save each day will increase to 20, and failure to save will inflict disadvantage to wisdom checks for the day as well as add to the addiction counter. failing 3 checks in the second phase will progress the addiction to phase 3.
The final stage of the addiction, stage 3, will result in player needing to do a DC 25 wisdom save each day. if they succeed, they have disadvantage to wisdom checks for the day and take 1d12 poison damage. if they fail, their wisdom, strength and constitution is reduced by 5 for the day until they take more potion. upon taking the potion, they take 1d12 damage and heal 1d4. they have disadvantage on wisdom and constitution saves.
