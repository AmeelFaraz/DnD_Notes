#### First Shard
The first shard will unlock the Patron's Touch cantrip.  this will enable the user to force a DC 10 wisdom save on a creature after hitting it with a melee attack. on a fail, nothing will happen. on a success, deal additional 1d6 necrotic damage and heal half of the damage dealt
#### Second Shard
Obtaining a second shard will upgrade the cantrip. the wisdom save will increase to a DC 13. If the creature is damaged/below 50 HP and rolls a nat 1 for the wisdom save, it will instantly die.
#### Third Shard
Getting a third shard will upgrade the cantrip further. the wisdom save will increase to a DC 15. If a creature rolls a nat 1 on the save and instantly dies, it will be re-animated in the servitude of its killer for 10 turns.

After obtaining the third shard, the [[Mysterious Patron]] will make contact with the one that serves it in a time of great stress and offer help in exchange for something. The boon received will be equivalent to what is sacrificed. the options for sacrifice are -2 to a stat, a spell (reward improves depending on level), or magic items.
#### Fourth Shard
The Fourth shard will improve the save DC to be 16. Three times per long rest, the caster can force a failure of the wisdom saving throw (this will not trigger the instant death, it will simply get you extra damage and a heal)
#### Fifth Shard
The Fifth shard will improve the cantrip by improving the duration of a turned ally to be 1 day (after something gets a nat 1 save, it will last for a day as opposed to 10 turns).
