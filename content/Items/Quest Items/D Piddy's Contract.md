### **The Binding Agreement of Presence and Presentation**

**By Authority of : D. Piddy, Licensed Operator of Effery Jepstein Enterprises - Ramsted Branch**

Let it be known that the following parties (hereafter referred to as _The Associates_) do hereby enter into contract with D. Piddy, K.Relly and Spevin Kacey (hereafter referred to as _The Proprietors_) under the following terms, conditions, and enchantments:

---

**Clause I – Primary Obligation**  
The Associates shall, during designated hours of commerce, remain within the personal office of The Proprietors, assuming positions of looming presence and striking menace, hereafter referred to as _the Presentation_. The Associates are to nod, grunt, or otherwise provide visible assent to the statements and propositions of The Proprietors, thereby reinforcing their perceived authority and reputation.

---

**Clause II – Prohibition of Harm**  
The Associates shall not, by hand, spell, weapon, or willful intent, directly cause harm to The Proprietors. For the avoidance of doubt, this clause covers physical, magical, and overtly hostile actions.

---

**Clause III – Conduct of Silence**  
The Associates shall remain silent during The Presentation, speaking no words that would contradict, undercut, or embarrass The Proprietors before clients or rivals. Thereafter the associates shall be allowed to speak but not speak any words that would contradict, undercut or embarrass the proprietors before clients and rivals

---

**Clause IV – Duration of Service**  
This Agreement shall remain binding until such time as The Proprietors declares their objectives of influence “satisfactorily reinforced,” or until such time as The Proprietors no longer conduct business in their current office.
