Plate armor.
Once per short rest, you can cast shield(no need for spell slot or knowing the spell).
Friendly creatures within 10ft of the wearer gain +2 AC.
Friendly creatures within 5ft of the wearer gain an extra bonus action. Once per long rest as an action you can consume all bonus actions from yourself and everyone within 10ft of yourself to give everyone within 10ft an extra action.
