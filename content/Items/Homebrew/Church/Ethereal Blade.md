+2 longsword. 
Once per short rest as a bonus action, make hits with the sword give 2d6 temp hp to the wielder.
###### Glassify
As an action, target a creature. It has to make a dc 15 constitution saving throw. If it fails, it takes your intelligence stat as damage, its move speed is halved, and it is rendered ethereal meaning it takes no physical damage but all magic damage involving them is doubled (both incoming and outgoing). The target also cannot make direct physical attacks. This effect lasts for 3 turns, with an attempt to save to end the effect early each turn. 
On a save, the target takes half damage and is slowed for a turn. 
Friendly targets can choose to fail the save and will take no damage (but will still be slowed)
