#improvable #attuned #weapon #action #bonus_action 

Once per short rest, you can imbue your bullets with power adding an additional 2d6 thunder damage to the target and creatures within 10ft of it. The added damage ignores resistances. 
Can be reloaded with a bonus action. 
Wielder gains resistance to thunder and lightning damage. 
Once per short rest, if the revolver has hit atleast 3 different enemies, you can use your action to begin concentrating on chained lighting: enemies hit have to make dex saves or they take 4d8 lighting damage and lose an action (not a turn). Effect lasts 5 turns and goes away if concentration breaks
