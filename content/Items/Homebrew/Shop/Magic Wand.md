#improvable #attuned #artifact #action 

Once per long rest, heal yourself or anything within a 15ft radius for 1d4 HP per charge the item holds. 
The item starts with 3 charges every day, and gains a charge whenever an enemy casts a spell within 60ft. 
All charges are expended in a use, any extra HP gained will be converted to temp hit points.
The item has a maximum of 10 charges
