#attuned #weapon #action 

+2 dagger.
Target a creature to make a wisdom saving throw with a DC of 16. On fail, they take 2d4 slashing damage and are afflicted with a bleed stack. Take 2d4 slashing damage at the end of your turn for every stack of bleed. Bleed stacks can be removed with healing.
On a crit strike or a crit fail of wisdom save, target takes an additional d6 of either fire or lightning damage.
