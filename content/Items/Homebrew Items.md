## Shop Items
### Force Coins 
#consumable #weapon #action 
Can store force damage equivalent of 1 action's worth of damage from everyone in active party. A fully charged force coin is dangerous as any vigorous movement may set it off. They act as impact dials from One Piece 

### Magic Wand
#improvable #attuned #artifact #action 
Once per long rest, heal yourself or anything within a 15ft radius for 1d4 HP per charge the item holds. The item starts with 3 charges every day, and gains a charge whenever an enemy casts a spell within 60ft. All charges are expended in a use, any extra HP gained will be converted to temp hit points. The item has a maximum of 10 charges 

### Gambler’s Delight 
#improvable #weapon #action
Fires an eldritch blast + your main stat as damage. Roll a d4 and call odds or evens. Getting it wrong will cause user to take the damage instead. 

### Levitating Rope
#artifact 
10m long length of rope. Will levitate in place at the command word ‘freeze’. Cannot support more than 150 lbs of weight

### Shadowspain
#improvable #attuned #weapon #action #bonus_action 
+5 Dagger. When you kill something, store a charge up to 5 charges. You can use a charge to teleport anywhere in a 60m radius as a bonus action. Once per long rest you can use an action to go invisible. This weapon will return to your hand after it is thrown or if you have been disarmed. 


### Thunderstruck Revolver
#improvable #attuned #weapon #action #bonus_action 
Once per short rest, you can imbue your bullets with power adding an additional 2d6 thunder damage to the target and creatures within 10ft of it. The added damage ignores resistances. 
Can be reloaded with a bonus action. 
Wielder gains resistance to thunder and lightning damage. 
Once per short rest, if the revolver has hit atleast 3 different enemies, you can use your action to begin concentrating on chained lighting: enemies hit have to make dex saves or they take 4d8 lighting damage and lose an action (not a turn). Effect lasts 5 turns and goes away if concentration breaks 


### Assault Cuirass
#improvable #attuned #armor #action 
Plate armor.
Once per short rest, you can cast shield(no need for spell slot or knowing the spell).
Friendly creatures within 10ft of the wearer gain +2 AC.
Friendly creatures within 5ft of the wearer gain an extra bonus action. Once per long rest as an action you can consume all bonus actions from yourself and everyone within 10ft of yourself to give everyone within 10ft an extra action. 


### Ethereal Blade 
#improvable #attuned #weapon #action #bonus_action 
+2 longsword. 
Once per short rest as a bonus action, make hits with the sword give 2d6 temp hp to the wielder.
###### Glassify
As an action, target a creature. It has to make a dc 15 constitution saving throw. If it fails, it takes your intelligence stat as damage, its move speed is halved, and it is rendered ethereal meaning it takes no physical damage but all magic damage involving them is doubled (both incoming and outgoing). The target also cannot make direct physical attacks. This effect lasts for 3 turns, with an attempt to save to end the effect early each turn. 
On a save, the target takes half damage and is slowed for a turn. 
Friendly targets can choose to fail the save and will take no damage (but will still be slowed)

## Quest Rewards

### Amulet of the Wyrd - Tarrasque Infusion
#improvable #attuned #artifact #bonus_action #action 
+2 to attack  & damage while raging.
One extra resistance when raging, decided at the start of the rage. 
At the start of the rage, gain temporary hit points equal to your constitution modifier plus your barbarian level.
#### Savage Transformation 
*The Soul of The Wrath flows through you*

Use a bonus action to enter beast form. Lose control of what you attack and aggro the closest creature to you.
+1d6 to all damage, Deal double damage to contructs.
Jump distance is tripled, +30ft of movement.
Advantage on Strength checks, cannot be charmed or mind controlled.
After landing a finishing blow on anything, you will be bloodlusted making hit attacks do maximum damage for 5 turns. Threshold to crit with a bite will be decreased by the number of final blows achieved in the fight. 
If you are unable to get a final blow during the fight, The Wrath will reach through time and sate its hunger by using Titanic Maw on anything within range or that it can reach within 1 turn of movement. If it cannot reach anything, then the damage is inflicted on yourself
###### Titanic Maw
Once per rage you can make a special bite attack rolling a d20 for damage. Criting with this attack will instantly kill any creature smaller than your current size. 
###### Beast Roar
As a bonus action, everything in a 50ft radius must make a wisdom saving throw, DC 8 + const mod + proficiency bonus. On fail, frightened for 3 turns and you have advantage on it.
