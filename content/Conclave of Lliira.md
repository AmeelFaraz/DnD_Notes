The followers of Lliira, Goddess of Joy. This particular sect follows the teachings of the great sage Farzameel and his central idea that true joy can only be achieved from the dota prayer

---
## Lore

#### Description
The cult formed around the worshipping of Lliira. Found in pockets all around the world, the Lliirans have developed a reputation for being good natured and helpful, as their god loves joy and the joyful. Conclaves of Lliira therefore are often known to go to areas stuck by tragedy or hardship to help out and bring joy to people as their form of worship.

## Goals

The original goal of the Conclave as a whole is to recover all the steps of the dota prayer to unlock the secret of eternal joy
