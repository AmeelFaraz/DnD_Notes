A pair of magical doors that appear in the center of the [[Charred Blast Zone]].  Can only be seen with truesight. they guard the entrance to  [[Kuyob's Pocket Dimension]], the only way to enter the [[Tower of Kuyob]]. 

There are several sets of doors all over the world (and perhaps this universe) and kuyobs has a personable relationship with each one.
