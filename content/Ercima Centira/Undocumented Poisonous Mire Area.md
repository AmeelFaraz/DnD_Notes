A marshy, swampy land to the north of [[Ramsted]]. Largely ignored by the city as it has historically been deemed a noxious backwater with little to offer...but is that truly the case?

---
## Lore

#### Description

The land becomes softer and wetter are you approach the poisonous mire. The sparse grassland stops, and a mass of underbrush and thickets of unfriendly thorny bushes and snarls of once living plant matter line the area as far as the eye can see. the damp dank ground seems to have forgotten what a road is supposed to look like.
