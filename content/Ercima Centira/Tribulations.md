A contest and series of challenges, both mental and physical, done by all Acolytes of the Grand Church of Science after their first week at the [[Grand Church of Science]]. 

The grueling tests determine each individuals worth and how they would be best suited to serving science, and confer the ranks of Searcher, Seeker, and Converter.

The true goal of the tribulations is to determine who would be best fit to be a [[Grand Church of Science Hierarchy#Scalpels of the Grand Church of Science|Scalpel]].
