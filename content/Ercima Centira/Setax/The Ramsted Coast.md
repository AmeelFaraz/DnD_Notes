The Ramsted Coast contains a collection of minor settlements and one larger village, [[stelvongal]]. The area is demarcated as the area from the ocean up to the natural barrier made by [[The Twin Wood]]. The Ramsted Coast’s economy and livelihood are mostly dependent on fishing and trading with vessels that come to it in search of a hospital or reasonable renown, [[The Orme House of Healing]]. 

As of recent, the fishing and general livelihood of the people in the smaller fishing villages on the coast has been put to ruin due to a string of ferocious Sahaugin attacks. This was caused as a result of mind control of fellow sahaugins at the hands of [[The Astral Kraken]], which was attempting to exercise its abilities to raise an army so that it might set up a lair in the nearby area containing the [[Sahaugin Cavern]].
