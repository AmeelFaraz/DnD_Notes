The core city and area the campaign takes place in

---
[[Ramsted]] is a city in the land of [[Setax]]. It is in the western part of the continent, amongst lands generally considers to backwaters and not of much note. Ramsted is in a position of some logistical import as it is built relatively close to the ocean, just a two days walk from [[The Ramsted Coast]]. it is hedged in near the south by [[The Hundred Hills]], ringed by woodlands such as [[The Twin Wood]] and crowned by the [[Undocumented Poisonous Mire Area]] to the north. 
![[Ramsted Area Map.jpeg]]
Recently, the landscape was altered by a powerful blast that charred the area, making an already barren patch of land even more so. Scouting parties reported back not much of note, saying that the [[Charred Blast Zone]] did not contain any space metals or other minerals. 

![[Ramsted City Map.jpeg]]

## Core Issue

Ramsted and its surrounding areas have been afflicted by an all encompassing and terrifyingly deadly [[Death Disease]]. The illustrious and ever loved [[Delmar Danth]] set out to find a cure to this disease, gathering a band of his finest friends and contacts to form [[The Party]] to aide him in this matter.

## Institutions and Organizations

###### Grand Church of Science 
Ramsted is home to quite a few organizations. Chief amongst these in terms of outwards power would be The [[Grand Church of Science]]. It has slowly taken over the city and its citizens, becoming the defacto leadership of the city. 

###### Conclave of Lliira
In direct conflict with and the strongest opposition to the GCS is the [[Conclave of Lliira]]. Another religious group, their followers sometimes clash with the scientists in the city, although this has been happening less frequently as of late. 

###### City Government
The [[City Government]] lost the support of the people a while ago, due to several problems. What is left of their fractured leadership continues on much subdued, their goals (and commonly, continued existence) are unknown to most in the city. 

###### The Wyrdkin
The [[Ramsted Sewers]] are cavernous, vast, and house all sorts of things including a group at odds with seemingly everyone on the surface. Known as The [[Wyrdkin]], this group of shapeshifting people/creatures has suffered under the rule of Humans for centuries.

###### Ramsted Ranch
Most publicly reclusive of all would be the [[Ramsted Ranch]]. Known as simply “The Ranch”, most people generally try to avoid it as the rumors swirling about of its purpose and happenings are…less desirable to be attached to one’s person.
