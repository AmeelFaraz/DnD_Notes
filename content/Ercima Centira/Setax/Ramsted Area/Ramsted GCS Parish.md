The Physical church for the religion of the [[Grand Church of Science]] in the [[Ramsted]] area

---
## Lore

#### Description
A 3 story tall structure, it is the highest and most imposing building in all of [[Ramsted]]. Its walls are completely black and two spires jut out from each of its corners and spiral into the sky. There is a gap in the middle of the structure which is the courtyard of the church, which holds a giant tree that appears long dead judging from its withered appearance. the back half of the church after the courtyard rises only 2 stories high compared to the three stories of the front part, the two areas connected by long skinny hallways that also serve as the courtyards outside facing walls.
The front doors are made of heavy reinforced wood some 20 ft high and require two well muscled guards to be able to pull them just enough apart to admit one regular sized person.
![[GCS Inspo Image.png]]
#### Other
The courtyard of the church holds a statue of the prophet xarrod, under which there is a passageway into an underground bunker which houses the [[Body of Science]]. There is an elephants foot of radiation material as well as an actual nuclear weapon core
