The previously ramshackle house that was some government members hideout. It was taken by [[The Party]] as their defacto base of operations after assassinating the previous owner at [[Nightshade Shadowsbane]]’s request. 

It is in the [[Old Goverment District]] of [[Ramsted]]. 

The party hid [[Alayna Clorvus]] in here to keep her safe from anyone who may be interested in finding her

The safehouse location has been told/shown to [[Rosenbaum Lent]].
