## Lore

#### Description
A great resources for everyone who lives nearby, the woods are also host to a surprising number of magical creatures.

#### Other
The woods are of great importance to the [[Cloven Council]] due to their magical fauna

The woods are also home to [[Kin'Ra]] 

The animals of the twin woods have split into two political parties, the [[Open Skies Party]] and the [[Shaded Leaf Party]]. they work to elect the [[Viceroy of the Heart]], which holds a certain amount of power over the other animals
