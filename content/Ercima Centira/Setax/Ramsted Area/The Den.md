## Lore

#### Description
Hidden in the maze-like network of twisting paths and illusory distractions that make up the [[Ramsted Sewers]], the Den is a safehouse-turned command outpost for the leaders and staunchest supporters of the city government. 

The rank, slimy, moldering paths that make up the sewers are certainly not clean or particularly safe by any stretch of the imagination. Even the most often used paths and entrances will cause workers to hold their noses and steel their stomachs with thoughts of their loved ones dependence on them to get through their daily toils. Yet even in this realm of fetid wastes and refuse there remain nooks and crannies that collect the worst of it all. Old abandoned passages and half dug expansion projects choke with the worst of the malodorous, putrid contents that the sewers were made to house and hopefully flush out. Indeed, even in the lowest of places, grime tends to distribute itself away from thoroughfares. 
A ways from the branching tunnels and layers that make up the core of [[Immasi]]'s zone of influence is a cleft in the wall, revealing a opening that shows the abandoned beginnings of yet another service tunnel. A horrible smell escapes from the crack, one that could leave even the most hardened of sewer dweller reeling with their eyes a-water. Squeezing past the damaged edifice 'rewards' you with the source of the horrible smell. The walls, dirt and unfinished, match the roof some 9 feet up. A few feet below the ceiling: a pipe perhaps tall enough to crawl through overflowing with the worst looking and smelling sludge in all the realms, the vile contents spilling out creating a mephetic delta caking the floor at least 3 feet deep. 
The opening does its job exceptionally well, as even the most maleficent malcontents would not loiter in the area for long. This, of course, is excellent for the City Government as if anyone did spend too long in the area they might be able to spot the cleverly disguised illusion making the size of the pipe almost half as large as it truly was. 

#### Other
This space was created as a safehouse by the ranch for some of their smuggling operations. It was revealed to [[Rosenbaum Lent]] and [[Venka Arton]] by the ranch and they were the ones to suggest using it after [[Jonathan Clorvus]]'s untimely demise.
