Ever expanded and unmonitored these septic cisterns criss-cross under everything in the city, allowing for discrete meeting places and hidden lairs provided you can withstand the stench - and the occasional monster that goes squelch in the night...

---
The sewers are currently home to the [[Wyrdkin]], as well as [[The Den]], which is the hiding place of [[Madam Clorvus]]. 

The [[Basogre's Lair]] is also found here.

Due to the nature of their purpose, sewer entrances are found all over the city.
