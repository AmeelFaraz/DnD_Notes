## Lore

#### Description
A large long Ranch house near the north side of Ramsted. Notably, this is the same side of the city that houses the Government District as well as the [[Ramsted GCS Parish|Church Building]]. The building is surrounded by farmlands, all of which are owned by the ranch itself. There is a specific path from the northern city gate to the ranch. This path is a bit more well packed than the supposed main path from which it branches. a lone skinny path goes deeper into the heart of the Ranch, with a wrought iron fence overgrown with hedges shrouding the inside of the compound from the outside. 

While the Ranch is ostensibly a place to relax with a nudge and a wink towards more hedonistic pass times, most people are unaware of what truly transpires there
#### Other
The ranch is a bit of a front. hiding behind the gambling and the carnal indulgence is a cabal of powerful figures that control the happenings of the city. They gather dirt or other leverage against leaders of the other factions which allows them to push their own agendas. The agenda includes gaining even more power and influence as well as abusing kids. While some of the kids are orphans from the city that have no where to go or taken from the [[The Orme House of Healing]] after the parents unfortunate medical incidents, several others are stolen or taken away as leverage or just to satisfy the sick twisted leaders of the ranch. They also run [[Infernal Backdoor]]s which is why they have many dealings with avernus dwellers

The leaders of the ranch are [[D Piddy]], [[K Relly]], and [[Spevin Kacey]]. They answer to someone far above them who controls these hubs of trafficking and horrors, the shadowy and dangerous [[Effery Jepstein]]. 

They use [[Zida Games and Co]] machines for the front of house patrons

## Locations

#### Main Area
The inside of the ranch assaults all 5 sense, almost as if to make up for the dim austere facade outside. Brilliant lights of various colors suspended in tiny floating globes dazzle the eyes as they wink with flitting intensity. Thick plush carpets cover the floor, the intricate patterns made even more so by the dancing, kaleidoscopic lights. Jaunty music wafts through the air from a set of enchanted instruments that play away in a corner. The air is thick with the smell of perfumes mixed with smokes of various kinds which dampen and dim the view of the ornate ceiling. The center of the area is dominated by an ornate wooden cage open on all 4 sides. Game tables made of the same variety of wood flank the cage on either side till the edge of the large room. 
a few yards behind the Cage, the 'rear' of the room can be found to actually be a carved wooden divider wall with heavy saloon-style doors spaced sparingly along it. Each of these doors doesn't appear to be guarded by much more than young boys wearing butler/maid uniforms, but getting close to the doors instills a strong sense of unease, warding away any unauthorized individuals that may come too close.

The main area has opportunities for the party to gamble and perhaps pick up on some gossip. There will be no super high profile person here but maybe some higher ranking people from various factions. The cage cashier will be [[Gamjiv]] 

#### High Rollers Area
Beyond the wooden divider lies the high rollers area. A marked shift from the more public facing side in ambiance, this area has much more subdued lighting and colors. Not drab by any means, it moves away from the riotously bright and lively scheme to a more subtle, mature look. the carpet is a solid dark maroon, complemented by the rich deep browns of the divider wall and the open top 'rooms' that are found here. Unlike the open design of the main area, the high rollers area has several sequestered zones that act as rooms with walls that dont reach the ceiling around at least 3 of their sides. These more private areas are positioned so no one area can look directly into another. it would be a complete labyrinth were it not for the signs hanging from the ceiling with a number for each room. the area is split down the center and across the front in a sort of T shaped path which reveals an imposing looking large door on the back wall of the area. There are two more doors on either side of it that look more similar in design to the wooden walls of the high roller rooms. 

this place will have more important people and will allow for gambling 500+ gold on things.

#### The Back
The area which separates the casino portion of the ranch with the other places within it. The large metal doors open to show tile and stone rather than the carpet and wood of the previous areas. there are two corridors on either side, and a double set of stairs heading down past a desk area to places further beneath the building. 

#### The Showers
The light grey/white, shallowly pockmarked stone that makes up the walls and ceiling of the back area echoes with the sounds of water. The reflections of light on the water cast peculiar shadows and combined with the stone, give the feel of a shallow grotto or cave. the corridor continues on longer than seems possible given the estimate of how large the building is from the outside. Eventually, the corridor comes a turn, the path forward blocked off by frosted glass doors set in silvery metal. Torches give off muted light from behind similarly appointed sconces. 

#### The Sauna
A large room, around 25ft high. the walls are made of a blackend brick. the entrance to the area is blocked off by a door made of infernal magics. the door then displays a different set of markings. 3 empty dots at the top, with 9 sigils in a circle with a center. The following is written in infernal underneath the symbols "Hell is order, Order is law, law is deception. choose the path as it descends or be consumed"  following this, the two glowing red wrought iron covers on the door will bulge outward with a glow and melt away in the air, the edges curling inward before disappearing like a paper set to flame. ends with "what is the truest essence of Hell?" depending on who you serve you can only select the sigil of that layer/archdevil. 
The room is awash with a fiery glow and an oppressive heat emanating from 5 rips in reality floating in the air, in a sort of pentagon. black iron walkwaysgo from the ground up to each one. the room is split into two, the back half holding these portals, the wafting heat warping the images of the hells beyond. the front half of the room holds a singular ticket booth and an array of black iron fence that winds in an orderly fashion, like the security line at an airport.

#### The Offices
A suite of 3 extremely plush domiciles, the Offices of D Piddy, K Relly and Spevin Kacey are nothing short of kingly.
