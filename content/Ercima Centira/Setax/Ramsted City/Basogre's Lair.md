A staging ground for wrestling, feasting on Ill-begotten animals or people, and disgruntled plotting on how to best bash [[Immasi]]‘s head to let [[Basogre]] eat as many people as he desires. 

The lair is also used as a convenient safe house for [[Puss]] and his crew. 

Located in the lair is a staff that can call [[Basogre]] and his fellows into battle when required. This is actually a hidden Rod.
