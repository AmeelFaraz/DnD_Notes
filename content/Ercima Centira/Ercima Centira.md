The First World created in Ameel’s Homebrew DnD setting. While there are supposed to be multiple worlds, and even multiple dimensions, nothing is fleshed out to the level of [[Setax]] and more specifically, [[Ramsted]] and the surrounding areas which are in Western [[Setax]] . There is only one land mass in [[Ercima Centira]] but this has different ‘continents’ within it. The name is simply an anagram for America Center, as the lands are all supposed to be different parts of a post apocalyptic America, ravaged by a massive nuclear war 10,000 years ago. The war is known to the few who know about it (such as [[Grand Church of Science]] and [[Divine Avatar]]s) as [[The First Descent of Science]] .
