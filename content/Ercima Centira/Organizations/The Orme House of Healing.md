## Lore
#### Description
A fortified compound located a few miles off [[The Ramsted Coast]]. The complex is well maintained and the tasteful decorations and landscaping (such as a waist high hedge maze out front) speak to the higher-brow clientele that makes up the most of the house's patients. Inside the boundary walls (which are of modest height but would be no slouch in the event of a required defense) is the most striking part of the hospital :  the main dome with four proud towers rising up behind and beside it in a row. The particular angle of the gates of the entrance framed by the main building of the house and all 4 towers behind it is quite impressive to behold. The house docks are strategically placed to ensure coming patients or visitors see this sight when they arrive.
#### Other
[[The Orme House of Healing]] was created by an unknown person a number of years ago. it is in a unique position as it operates out of the jurisdictions of any government due to its highly defensible position of being on an island off the coast of [[Ramsted]]. It attracts all sorts of wealthy noble clients due to its reputation for solid work and not so modest price. however, the locals or poorer people within its area of service also think highly of it, as they maintain some spots dutifully reserved for the sole purpose of aiding those who have nothing and no one. 

The leadership of the house is noted in [[House of Healing Hierarchy]].
## Conflict/Quests
Outwardly, it seems that the House's goals are in line with what one would expect of a hospital: doing research and patient care. However, the goals of those who lead the house are mostly not so benign. 

The [[House of Healing Hierarchy#The General Doctor|General Doctor]] wants to heal people and was attracted to the role in hopes of serving a historically underserved region. 

The [[House of Healing Hierarchy#The Doctor of Finance and Fundraising|Doctor of Finance and Fundraising]] is corrupt and embezzling money. he is very suspicious of anyone related to monetary institutions. He has had dealings with [[The Izubar Counting House]] and is extremely wary of any auditing they might do. He intends to slowly increase prices and reduce positions for the poor to maximize money
- Together with the doctor of research, they are working with the [[Conclave of Lliira]] to create and test [[Bottled Joy]] at the hospital (joy lab link here)

The [[House of Healing Hierarchy#The Doctor of Disease and Viruses|Doctor of Disease and Viruses]] is a necromancer similar to [[Necrolord Nyctasha]]. As an entity with a concentration of [[The Essence]], he is drawn to [[Mysterious Patron]] and is attempting a goal similar to what [[Necrolord Nyctasha]] has done (cause a large amount of death). He does so by killing patients that could perhaps have survived and aiding the [[House of Healing Hierarchy#The Doctor of Research and Experimental Medicine|Doctor of Research and Experimental Medicine]].
- The doctor of disease has been working with the [[Grand Church of Science]] and was responsible for helping them enhance the Inoculation Ritual

The [[House of Healing Hierarchy#The Doctor of Research and Experimental Medicine|Doctor of Research and Experimental Medicine]] has a vendetta against the [[Wyrdkin]]. He wants to use them to create new medicines and things as well as to create the horrific [[Amalgamation]]s to serve as enhanced soldiers. 
- Together with the doctor of disease, they work to find ways to synthesize wyrdkin materials into powerful magical elixirs.

## Upper Hospital
The visible parts of the house of healing, built on top of the island
### Main Waiting Area
the main area of the hospital when one enters. The desk is piloted by [[Sarjiv]]. allows access to the [[The Orme House of Healing#Financial Wing|Financial Wing]] as well as the General Wing. Also holds a secret entrance to the [[The Orme House of Healing#Lower Hospital|Lower Hospital]].

### Financial Wing
the first door to the right when entering the House. It holds the entrance to the [[House of Healing Hierarchy#The Doctor of Finance and Fundraising|Doctor of Finance and Fundraising]]'s office and [[Barjiv]]'s desk. It also has a store room of ledgers in the tower, some of which will show that the budgets are not balancing truthfully, indicating embezzlement.

### General Wing
The first door to the left when entering the House. The area is always much busier than any of the other wing sin the hospital as it is where all the people go. Constant motion and exasperated nurses are helped out to some degree by [[Carjiv]]. It has the doctors office at the top of its tower, which also holds a room holding ledgers of all patients the hospital has seen. there are notably many entries that say 'taken for research' and redacted.

### Disease Wing
The entrance requires one to make their way through the General Wing. Manning the desk for this wing is [[Narjiv]]. This wing also houses the Morgue at its far end. Similar to the other wings, it has a tower at its center point which houses the Doctor's office. This office is guarded at all times by a nurse that stands near the entrance to the tower. The ledger room in this tower contains lists of autopsy reports as well as certain parts for some entries highlighted 

### Research Wing
while there is a tower for the research wing and a part of the building that can be seen from the outside, this wing is never seen by any who are not explicitly involved with the House. The office at the top of the tower is real, however the only way to enter this tower is with a master keycard which is the combination of two doctor keycards. This will allow the user to access the hidden entrance to the [[The Orme House of Healing#Lower Hospital|Lower Hospital]], and through it the true research wing.
