## Lore

The Grand Church of Science was formed 9000 years ago, when the first prophet began to explain to the people of [[Ercima Centira]] what had occurred when the world changed in the new era. He explained that due to people worshipping all sorts of false treasonous gods, Science descended upon the land with a fury, cleansing the world of the unbelievers. Now Science has gone to slumber and requires his followers to bring together his body. once the [[Body of Science]] has been reconstructed, Science will finally descend upon the land again and bring salvation to his people and fury upon the evil.  

Leadership of the Church is noted in [[Grand Church of Science Hierarchy]]
## Goals

To reconstruct the body of science there must be quests sent out to to track it and bring it back to the church. There are suspected areas of sciences resting places around the [[Ramsted]] area which are to be investigated and processed.

The Archbishop [[Willium Nylus]] is playing a political game with the Cardinal and the Pope. He hopes to be promoted and moved to a more happening area

[[Deacon Clein]] is trying to expand his personal wealth and general influence by abusing church institutions to his own gain. he has his fingers in several other groups, all of which seem to be suspicious of him. 

[[Deacon Dahc]] is trying to pin down [[Deacon Clein]] for his several crimes including those against the church (such as embezzlement or being a possible traitor ). More theologically involved, he also seeks to further church's goals above all else.
## Quests/Conflicts 

The church seeks to be the sole power in a city so that it can use all of its resources to carry out quests to find the body of science. to this end, they wish to destroy all other factions of apparent power. In Ramsted, this would be the [[Conclave of Lliira]] and the [[City Government]]
- [[Deacon Clein]] succeeded in the latter, with the government being a splintered shell of what it once was

Obtain parts of the body of science and combine it with the larger [[Body of Science]] under the church.
- to this end, the Church may need a new (perhaps a few) [[Sanctified Maiden]].

Discover who or what has been directing attacks against the church, primarily in regards to the Lustcubus. 
- [[Deacon Dahc]] believes this to be the work of Clein, in league with outside forces
- Church rumors also point fingers at [[Ramsted Ranch]], as they are known to be 'demon worshippers'

The Church is now also intent on protecting the remaining 3 Relics in the library. they also want to know who has taken the relics so they may be brought to justice.
- Chief amongst the suspects is the unknown and strangely named John Lennon, who is wanted for impersonating an agent of a Deacon.
