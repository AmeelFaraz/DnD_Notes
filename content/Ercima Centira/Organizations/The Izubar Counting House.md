The oldest and most respected financial institution in [[Setax]] (and possibly the world, will have to see later), its money is always good and will be lent on time for reasonable rates. 

---

They cultivate this aura of trust by inculcating the fact of a heartless and unrelenting ability to make good on their investments by obtaining collateral - no matter what. 

> *"The counting house will lend you money for a better rate perhaps...but keep in mind that the worst **I** could do is kill you.."*                                              - *popular saying amongst local money lenders*
