
## General Hierarchy
### Doctors
The heads of the hospital. each of the 4 are responsible for a different aspect of the house and mostly stay out of the others ways. They are the highest authority in the hospital, even if they like to vaguely talk about a 'board of directors' when convenient.

### Head Nurse
The right hand man/woman of their respective doctor. The position is one that doubles as a personal secretary often, but more involved in the day to day activities of their particular wing as well as anything the doctor requires

### Nurse
Technically on the same level of authority as the Head Nurse, the regular nurses will often defer to what the Head Nurse says. Nurses run the House and are responsible for everything from stabilizing emergency patients to cleaning out bed pans. 

### Receptionists
One for each wing of the hospital, these short odd fellows all look the exact same yet have vastly varying personalities. Employees of the House, they are lowest in the chain of command but are still vested some imagined authority over patients by virtue of their position. Their odd behaviors further a sense of mystery regarding them. The current receptionists are [[Sarjiv]], [[Barjiv]], [[Narjiv]], and [[Carjiv]].

Role Specifics
## The General Doctor
The role with the highest turnover of the 4 Doctors of the House. It is the most isolated from the other roles, and is the face of the House. While well paid, there never seems to be a doctor that lasts here for more than a couple of years

## The Doctor of Finance and Fundraising
The position currently held by [[Ave Rishus]]. This position is meant to secure funding for the hospital as well as ensure facilities are kept up to date. All monetary decisions require the Doctors final approval before they are put into effect. 

## The Doctor of Disease and Viruses
The position once held by [[Ivebinr Aisendead]]. This position is meant to cure and find cures to diseases as well as figure out ways to prevent them. It also is responsible for the House morgue and doing medical autopsies

## The Doctor of Research and Experimental Medicine
The position held by [[Dolor Inflictus]]. This position is meant to find knew medical treatments for all sorts of problems as well as develop new and more effective solutions to previously used treatments. the position is also intended to make new medicines.
