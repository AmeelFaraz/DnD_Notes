## Lore

#### Description

A jagged, warped slash in the air. the edges of the suspended portal curl inward slightly, as though it were the burned edges of a paper. The image of the plane beyond waft and waves with an invisible heat.

#### Other

While temporary portal between dimensions can be opened by any mage of sufficient power, the lords of Hell do not care to police them, partly due to the flighty nature of mages of that stature and other magical protections to limit any influence they may have. 
However, An infernal backdoor is a more long term entrance, once that can be used multiple times after its creation. These are a primary cause of concern for the Archdemons, as significant parts of their power and wealth come from taxing[^1] the entrances to their domains.  As such, the lords of hell spend a fair but on border security to make sure all entrances are documented.
