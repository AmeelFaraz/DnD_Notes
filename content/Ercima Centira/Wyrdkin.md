The proud name of a proud class of peoples in [[Setax]]. Often times discriminated against due to their unique abilities to transform into animals, the [[Wyrdkin]] also get a bad rap due to fanciful stories of unsavory things that go bump in the night such as Werewolves and Vampires.  

---
## Lore

#### Description
A race of beings infused with the magic of [[The Essence]]. they can shapeshift and have three forms, a 'humanoid' form where they will look like any of the traditional DnD races, an animal form where they look like whatever animal they can shift into, and a hybrid form which is an intermediate point between the other two forms. Some wyrdkin will naturally present in a hybrid form and have to train a great deal to be able to transition to either other forms. Classic DnD races that are Wyrdkins include Ratfolk, Tabaxi, Arakokra, etc. if it looks like an animorph reject you know where it goes.

#### Other
Instrumental in the building of [[Ramsted]] an untold number of years ago, the [[Wyrdkin]] have long resented being treated as second class citizens. These tensions came to a head a few years ago, resulting in the [[Wyrdkin]] being ostracized from [[Ramsted]] almost completely, having to seek refuge underneath the very city whose walls their ancestors helped raise.
