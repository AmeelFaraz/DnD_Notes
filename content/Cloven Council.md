## Lore

#### Characterization
The Cloven Council is set in very traditional druidic ways, beholden to a creed of nonintervention or conflict, focusing on saving animals and the forest in as many ways as they can without going out and contacting non druids and such. Their main charge is to ensure the safety of the [[Heart of the Forest]] (even though they do not actually know where it is or anything).

They are suspicious of new ways of doing things. 

They are intellectuals and are into the scientific process and stuff even though they are all magic users and stuff. will talk about publishing papers and such (publishing a paper is getting the paper encircled into the main tree of the grove, basically an inscription into the bark)

#### Other
They claim that they speak for the Heart and follow its will but in reality most of these rituals are self serving nonsense.
